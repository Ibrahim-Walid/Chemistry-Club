Chemistry Club Plan 2025

The chemistry club is aimed to be better than international clubs. The main goal of my plan is to push the club to keep going and improving to be a well-known club, not only in Egypt but beyond any limits. The club’s content is planned to be informative, exciting, competitive, and increase the passion for the amazing world of chemistry, the science that has the ability to study very diminished particles that cannot be seen even by a light microscope. A passion for this field is required to dig deeper than any boundaries. For those who don’t have that passion, the club aims to develop theirs through various activities and competitions.

Admission

Due to the various activities planned to be applied to the club, it is expected that the number of members will be double that of the previous season. So, the selection should be considered so that the number shouldn’t exceed 4 times the number of mentors to ensure the learning quality. The ones who really show passion in the form will be accepted. If the number of applicants is less, they all will be accepted except anyone who shows carelessness.

Mentors Selection

A test will be made for the applied mentors from all the school not just the past members to choose the mentors for the next season. Then an interview will be made to ensure that the mentors will treat the members well and not be aggressive with them, in addition to ensuring that they have the ability to deliver the information easily. The expected number of mentors will be 5-9 mentors.

Scoring System

  • 5 points for attending the session or getting the second high score in each assignment.
  • 10 points for being the highest score in each assignment.
  • 5 points for getting the full mark in a 5-questions pop quiz which will be held in an unexpected time.
  • The mentors of each session will choose a member who is the most interactive and gain 10 points and the second place will get 5 points.
  • If a fund was applied, the highest score each month will gain a prize.
  • These grades will affect choosing the next management board.
  • An unattended session without an excuse will result in -30 points.
  • An unattended session with an excuse will result in -10 points.
  • An unattended session with extremely difficult circumstances will result in 0 points loss.
  • An unsubmitted assignment will result in -30 points.
  • An unsubmitted assignment with an excuse, whatever it is, will result in -10 points.
