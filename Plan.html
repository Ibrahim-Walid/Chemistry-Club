Chemistry Club Plan 2025

The Chemistry Club brings together students passionate about exploring the wonders of chemistry through experiments, research, and collaboration. Join us to ignite your curiosity and dive into the fascinating world of science.

Admission

Due to the various activities planned to be applied to the club, it is expected that the number of members will be double that of the previous season. So, the selection should be considered so that the number shouldn’t exceed 4 times the number of mentors to ensure the learning quality. The ones who really shown the their passion in the form will be accepted. If the number of applicants is less, they all will be accepted except anyone who shows careless.

Mentors Selection

A test will be made for the applied mentors from all the school not just the past members to choose the mentors for the next season. The expected number of mentors will be 5-9 mentors.

The Sessions

Session Topic Description
Session 1 Introduction An introductory session to kick off the club activities.
Session 2 Advanced Chemistry Dive into advanced topics with hands-on experiments.

Scoring System

  • 5 points for attending the session or getting the second high score in each assignment.
  • 10 points for being the highest score in each assignment.
  • 5 points for getting the full mark in a 5-questions pop quiz which will be held in an unexpected time.
  • The mentors of each session will choose a member who is the most interactive and gain 10 points and the second place will get 5 points.
  • If a fund was applied, the highest score each month will gain a prize.
  • These grades will affect choosing the next management board.
  • An unattended session without an excuse will result in -30 points.
  • An unattended session with an excuse will result in -10 points.
  • An unattended session with extremely difficult circumstances will result in 0 points loss.
  • An unsubmitted assignment will result in -30 points.
  • An unsubmitted assignment with an excuse, whatever it is, will result in -10 points.

Club Website

EChO

Since there is no well-known chemistry competition in Egypt that can be joined, the club will host the Olympiad in Egypt that will qualify the winners to go to IChO. This will be under the supervision of Cairo University. Although it needs too much planning, it worth.

Forensic Chemistry

sessions will be made to simulate crimes and according to what was studied, members should tell what tests to do or what conclusions can they made form the provided evidences. This will be creative and make them think out of the box.

Improving the Community

There will be identity that reflects the club. T-shirts will be made that carry the club logo and chemistry-themed stuff. In addition, we can print the club logo on mugs or any other things.

There, research techniques will be discussed and that will provide the members with knowledge about their final project and the research sessions. Another interesting place is the Nutty Science in Cairo. In addition to many others. This also can be done before the beginning of the season which will result in attracting more members.
