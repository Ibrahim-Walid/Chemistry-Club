About Us

The Chemistry Club is a community that gathers all who are passionate about chemistry at STEM High School for Boys - 6th of October. Here, general chemistry is explained. All the students are gathered twice a week in a session in which the mentors present the concepts. There is an interesting assignment once every two weeks to ensure the concepts are well understood. All of that is done through the kind relationship between mentors and members. All of us have the same goal which is exploring the amazing world of chemistry through an interesting journey.
